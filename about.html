About Me

I am Kelly Sunday Esegine, a Software Engineer and Java Backend Developer from Ethiope East Local Government Area of Delta State, Nigeria.

I hold a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science from the Federal University of Agriculture Abeokuta, graduating in 2010.

With over 10 years of professional IT experience and more than 5 years of software engineering experience, I specialize in designing and developing scalable backend systems, enterprise applications, RESTful APIs, cloud-native solutions, and microservices architectures.

My technical expertise includes Java, Spring Boot, PostgreSQL, Docker, Kubernetes, Microservices Architecture, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Git and GitHub.

I am passionate about solving real-world problems through technology and building reliable, secure, and maintainable software solutions.
